CONTROLS:
w,a,s,d - to move (arrow keys)
1,2,3,4 - shows the effects of a special attack not yet implemented
Click and drag - to de/ select nano-bits
Press the esc key while dragging to cancel selection

BUGS:
- You can’t select within the player’s trigger collider or trigger attack collider
- The nano bits will get confused sometimes due to the overlapping colliders and will repeatedly smash into the player
